    Overview of MixMatch

    When we listen to music, there are two ways:
    streaming through spotify or youtube music, downloading the music file and listening through groove, or using another music player. But every music player has some defects on it, either it has serious lagging issues, or UIs are deteriorated.
    So we came up with another music player and we have added many functions that we think would be handy.

    Main Functions

    Basic Functions

    Play, next song, pause, before current song, volume, etc

    Playlist

    You can create your own playlist and export/import the playlist. You can share your own playlist to your friends and family, or you can get other peopleâ€™s playlist.

    Metadata editing

    You can make your custom tags for your favorite songs, or add in some comments to your songs. You can also change the metadata for any other purposes.

    Searching

    The search algorithm is based upon many things, could be the name of the song, or tags you created and metadata.

    Statistics

    You can figure your statistics and see what your favorite songs are.

    Styles

    You can change the styles of UI in the settings.

    Dark theme

    You can set the player to dark themes which are provided in the settings.
